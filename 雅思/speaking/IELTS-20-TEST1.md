# IELTS-20-TEST1

## PART1
- **How much walking do you do in your daily life?**
```
I don't really keep track of how much I walk every day. My routine is quite simple -- I walk about 10 minutes to the 
subway station on my way to work. However, I'm a bit overweight and have high blood pressure. My doctor advised me to 
exercise regularly. As a result, I try to go for a 30-minute walk twice a week.
```

- **Did you walk more when you were at school than now?**
```
Yes, definitely. I walked much more when I was at school than I do now. During the summer holidays, I often went out 
with my friends, and we would walk to different lakes to go fishing. Now my routine is much simpler, and most of my 
walking is just on my way to and from work.
```

- **What places are there to go for a walk near where you live?**
```
There is a school near where I live, and I often walk around it in the evening. I used to book the playground through 
the school's app, but it isn't always available because of school events. I prefer walking outside the school now 
because the area feels more open and lively, and there are often other people exercising or dancing nearby.
```

- **Would you ever like to go on a walking holiday?**
```
Yes, I would. My daily life is quite repetitive because I spend most of my time working or studying, even at weekends. 
So sometimes I need a proper break. I think a walking holiday by the coast would be ideal because I could see the sea, 
feel the breeze and listen to the waves. That would really help me relax.
```
